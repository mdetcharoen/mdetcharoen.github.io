About

I am a researcher in evolutionary genomics and molecular ecology. My work focuses on insect-microbe interactions, population genetics, and the application of high-throughput sequencing in evolutionary biology. I am currently based at the Division of Biological Science, Faculty of Science, Prince of Songkla University.
